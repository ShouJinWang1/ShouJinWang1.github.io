Hello World

Shoujin Wang

Research Fellow, RMIT University.

Casual Academic, Macquarie University.

Visiting Fellow, University of Technology Sydney.

Email: shoujin.wang@mq.edu.au

Shoujin Wang has been working as a research fellow at the department of computing, Macquarie University, Sydney, Australia under the supervision of Prof. Yan Wang, Prof. Michael Sheng and Prof. Mehmet Orgun . Prior to joining Macquaire, Shoujin obtained his PhD in data science from University of Technology Sydney in 2018 under the supervision of Prof. Longbing Cao and Dr. Wei Liu. His main research interests include data mining, machine learning and recommender systems. He has published more than 30 research papers and most of them were published at CORE Rank A*/A conferences or journals and JCR Q1 journals. Typical publication venues include premier AI conferences such as WWW, IJCAI, AAAI, ECML-PKDD and prestigious journals such as ACM Computing Surveys (CSUR), IEEE Transactions on Systems, Man, and Cybernetics: Systems and also has served as a PC at international conferences including AAAI, IJCAI, PAKDD and a reviewer for prestigious journals including machine learning, IEEE intelligent systems, IEEE transactions on Cybernetics.

Shoujin is actively seeking an academic position in Australia, if you think he is a potential candidate, please contact him via shoujin.wang@mq.edu.au.
