Research

Research Interest:

Shoujin's research investigates novel data mining and machine learning techniques and their applications, with a research theme focused on user behavior analysis and modelling, recommender systems, fake news mitigation. His long-term goal is to design intelligent and reliable recommender systems to provide trustworthy and responsible recommendation services to end users in the complex cyber space which is full of threat from noise, bias, attacks, etc. The outcomes are expected to facilitate the development of the digital economy and sustainable society. Some of his focused research topics include:

(1). Pattern mining and knowledge discovery

(2). User behavior analysis and modeling

(3). Recommender systems

(4). Fake news detection and mitigation
